IMPECCABLE WAFFLE RECIPE

HERE ARE THE INSTRUCTIONS TO MAKING THE BEST WAFFLES

  • 1 cup flour
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 5 sprinkles of cinnamon ground
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • Big splash of vanilla essence
  • 28 grams (2 tablespoons) melted butter

PUT THE DRY INGREDIENTS (the first 7 ingredients) IN A LARGE BOWL AND MIX THEM TOGETHER. IN A SEPARATE BOWL, MIX THE WET INGREDIENTS (ingredients 8, 9, and 10). COMBINE ALL OF THAT INTO ONE BOWL AND MIX UNTIL THERE ARE NO LUMPS. ADD THE MELTED BUTTER AND STIR.
